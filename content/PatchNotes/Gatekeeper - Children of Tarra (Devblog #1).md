# Gatekeeper - Children of Tarra (Devblog #1)

<b>Hello, Gatekeepers!</b><br /><br />We’ve finally decided to start a dev blog, and this first series will be dedicated to the upcoming update, “Children of Tarra.”<br /><br /><i>A little nervous, but I’ll manage… Ahem.</i><br /><br />Today, we’ll talk about a new Gatekeeper and fixes for some critical issues.<br /><br /><div class="bb_h2">1. Void Model</div><img src="https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/42755050/4e53d51e302d6a7b3e74331c6e5ede7d0ef86906.png" /><br /><br />Void Model is our new pride and, we hope, your next favorite melee Gatekeeper.<br /><br />Currently, it’s the most mobile Gatekeeper, with its key feature being a passive ability to infect enemies with Entropy.<br /><br />Each stack of Entropy reduces the enemy’s damage and movement speed, making them more vulnerable to the already deadly Void Model.<br /><br /><b>Skill Set:</b><ul class="bb_ul"><li> <b>Void Claws</b> Attacks with claws at medium range: two quick strikes, followed by a pause, then a powerful attack with a dash forward, applying Entropy.<br /></li><li> <b>Ravage</b> Dashes forward, passing through enemies and dealing damage in a line. Gains +3% attack speed for every enemy hit, lasting 4 seconds.<br /></li><li> <b>Fade</b> Enters an incorporeal state, dealing damage over time (DoT) to enemies around for 3 seconds.<br /></li><li> <b>Entropy Release</b> Deals damage to all enemies infected with Entropy. Damage increases by 20% for each stack of Entropy.<br /></li><li> (Passive) <b>Entropy Touch</b> Spreads Entropy. Each stack of Entropy reduces enemy damage and movement speed by 1% (up to 20 stacks).</li></ul>As Void Model evolves, more options for spreading and controlling Entropy will become available.<br />We believe fans of fast-paced AoE gameplay will find this Gatekeeper particularly exciting!<br /><br /><div class="bb_h2">2. Crashes, Infinite Loading Screens, Softlocks, etc.</div>Since the major update released on October 7, 2024, we’ve received numerous reports of issues (most notably, the infamous <i><b>LOADING SCREEN OF DEATH</b></i>).<br /><br /><img src="https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/42755050/cb4cf2c4519f1510596cb747add94acaf66ed46e.png" /><br />We’re happy to announce that 95% of these issues have been resolved!<br /><br />We’ve been working closely with the developers of the multiplayer plugin we use, and they’ve been diligently fixing the bugs affecting Gatekeeper over the past few months.<br /><br />Another issue reported by some players is the loss of save files after an emergency shutdown (e.g., power outages or critical Windows errors). We’re redesigning the save system and introducing <b>backups</b>.<br />In the future, if your save file gets corrupted, the system will automatically restore it from a backup.<br /><br />Additionally, we’ve reworked the controllers for all Gatekeepers, making them more responsive. For instance, you can now start sprinting immediately after an attack rather than waiting 1–2 seconds. We’ve also fixed many minor bugs in their skills.<br /><br />That’s all we wanted to share today.<br />This dev blog series will consist of four parts. The second part will be released after the New Year and will discuss the new Elite Enemy system.<br /><br />For now, our team is taking a short one-week break to recharge before the final sprint toward the release of “<i>Children of Tarra</i>” update.<br /><br /><i>We wish you a wonderful holiday season!</i><br /><b>Gravity Lagoon</b><br /><br /><div class="bb_h2">Socials</div><a class="bb_link" href="https://steamcommunity.com/linkfilter/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fdiscord.gg%2Fgravitylagoon" rel=" noopener" target="_blank">Join our Discord</a><br /><a class="bb_link" href="https://www.reddit.com/r/Gatekeeper/" rel="" target="_blank">Join our Subreddit</a><br /><a class="bb_link" href="https://steamcommunity.com/linkfilter/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fx.com%2Fgatekeeper_game" rel=" noopener" target="_blank">Follow on X</a>
