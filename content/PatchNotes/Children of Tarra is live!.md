# Children of Tarra is live!

We hope you enjoy Palium and all it has to offer. Players can now block a limited number of artifacts from appearing in Obelisks during a run. Each ban costs 5 Omni Stones.<br /> • Enemy level scaling has been revamped for a smoother and fairer difficulty curve.<br /> • Artifact descriptions have been fully redesigned for better readability.<br /> • Singleplayer mode added to the network menu, allowing offline play.<br /> • Paths from Chaos Wards to their areas are now shown as moving dashed lines, making navigation easier.<br /> • Sprint can now be triggered immediately after attacking (previously had a short delay).<br /> • Cursed Artifact descriptions for Mediator now display both old and new values for better clarity of the passive ability.<br /><div class="bb_h2">Mechanics Changes</div> • Certain artifact stats now scale logarithmically for improved balance.<br /> • Bleed effect reworked. Damage is now dealt once per damage tick, regardless of the number of Bleed stacks. Tick frequency depends on the Gatekeeper’s attack speed. <br /> •  No more elemental damage bonuses from Bleeding, but overall damage has been increased.<br /> • Tech Hunter’s first skill evolution now applies Bleed with a 15% chance instead of guaranteed.<br /> • Final damage calculations have been improved, accounting for various buffs and debuffs on both Gatekeepers and enemies.<br /> • Gatekeepers can now be affected by Burn and Bleed (yes, it’s going to hurt).<br /> • Powered enemies (with green outlines) have been removed and replaced by Elite enemies.<br /> • Kettlebell artifact now has a stronger knock-back <br /> • Drones no longer spawn in the Quiet Bay.<br /><div class="bb_h2">Bug Fixes & Stability Improvements</div>• Fixed: Sirens and Rampants on the Arena could reach excessively high HP values.<br />• Fixed: Players could sometimes get stuck on an infinite loading screen when entering a location.<br />• Fixed: A large number of reconnect-related issues that could occur when a player rejoined a run.<br />• Fixed: Audio overloads and distortions caused by extremely high enemy counts.<br />• Fixed: Changing graphics presets too drastically could sometimes cause the game to freeze or even crash.<br />• Save System Overhaul: Previously, if a computer lost power unexpectedly during gameplay, save files could become corrupted. Now, the game automatically creates a backup of the save file.
