# Children of Tarra (DevBlog #4)

Greetings Gatekeepers!<br /><br />It’s time for the fourth and final part of our Children of Tarra Update devblog.<br />Today, we’re covering all the exciting additions that didn’t make it into the previous three entries!<br /><br /><div class="bb_h3">Enemy Growth Indicator</div>You’ll now always be able to see the current level of enemies and how quickly they’re getting stronger.<br />The growth speed depends on the selected difficulty level.<br /><br /><img src="https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images/42755050/998603b8c9dfb9d6f0da249e0961e98e44f84f57.jpg" /><br /><br /><div class="bb_h3">Updated Artifact Descriptions</div>We’ve completely reworked artifact descriptions!<br />Previously, they were long blocks of text that made it difficult to quickly understand their effects—especially in the heat of battle.<br /><br /><img src="https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images/42755050/b628ff66f9993e8526b31784f2383668fa83b3ad.jpg" /><br /><br />Now, descriptions are split into two sections:<br /> • Short Description – Explains the artifact’s core effect.<br /> • Parameters – Displays the numerical values of its effects.<br />We’ve also improved Mediator’s artifact descriptions to make parameter changes more visually clear.<br /><br /><img src="https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images/42755050/7cf75764d81132417ae4e9c0f2a46148348e9e8a.png" /><br /><br /><div class="bb_h3">Emporium</div> • The Emporium has been moved to Delta, allowing players to unlock new artifacts mid-run.<br /> • You can now ban specific artifacts in the Emporium during gameplay.<br /><br /><img src="https://clan.fastly.steamstatic.com/images/42755050/b28995754920b43dc0fe61426d5eb69da213907b.png" /><br /><br /><div class="bb_h3">Online Play</div> • A Singleplayer Mode has been added, allowing players to play completely offline.<br /> • Many major issues have been fixed, including infinite loading screens, frequent desyncs, and other bugs. Unfortunately, some problems may still occur, so please report them as soon as possible. We’re doing everything we can to ensure a stable experience by the time version 1.0 launches!<br /><br /><div class="bb_h3">What’s New?</div> • At least four new interactive objects are coming to the new locations, unlocking fresh content.<br /> • Some of Tarra’s abilities have been buffed, and she’s gained a brand-new skill.<br /> • The length of a full loop has been increased by one extra planet.<br /> • Three new soundtracks, composed specifically for the atmosphere of Palium.<br /> • 13 new Steam achievements.<br /> • Gatekeepers can now burn and bleed!<br /><br /><div class="bb_h3">What’s Next?</div>We’re now shifting focus to the next major update, which will introduce the final planet—Crustad.<br /><br />But before that, we’re also planning to:<br /> • Update the Arena with more buildcrafting options.<br /> • Add new effects for Chaos Wards.<br /> • Introduce new Triads.<br /> • Overhaul the final stats screen, adding new parameters and allowing players to view their teammates’ stats.<br /><br />See you on Palium!<br />Gravity Lagoon <br /><br /><div class="bb_h2">Socials</div><a class="bb_link" href="https://steamcommunity.com/linkfilter/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fdiscord.gg%2Fgravitylagoon" rel=" noopener" target="_blank">Join our Discord</a><br /><a class="bb_link" href="https://www.reddit.com/r/Gatekeeper/" rel="" target="_blank">Join our Subreddit</a><br /><a class="bb_link" href="https://steamcommunity.com/linkfilter/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fx.com%2Fgatekeeper_game" rel=" noopener" target="_blank">Follow on X</a>
